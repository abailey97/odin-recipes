steak

description

ribeye steak is a type of steak that is cut from the rib section of a cow. It is a heavily marbled cut of beef, which means it has visible flecks or streaks of fat running throughout the meat. The marbling helps to keep the steak tender and juicy during cooking, as the fat melts and infuses the meat with flavor. When cooked properly, a ribeye steak should have a crispy, caramelized exterior and a juicy, pink or red center that is slightly marbled with fat. It is a flavorful and rich cut of meat that is best enjoyed in moderation as part of a balanced diet.

ingredients

  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 12oz ribeye
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • rosemary
  • thyme
  • salt and pepper

steps

  1. season steak with salt and pepper, heat cooking oil in cast iron over high heat
  2. once oil is shimmering add steak
  3. cook until until golden brown on one side then flip
  4. after flipping immediately add butter, garlic, rosemary, thyme
  5. baste with butter until golden brown on bottom
  6. remove from pan and rest for five minutes before serving
