cacio_e_pepe

description

Cacio e pepe is a classic Italian pasta dish that translates to "cheese and pepper" in English. It originated in Rome and is made with just a few simple ingredients: spaghetti, Pecorino Romano cheese, black pepper, and pasta water. Despite its simplicity, cacio e pepe is a beloved dish in Italy and around the world for its delicious, comforting flavors and creamy texture. It can be served as a main dish or as a side dish alongside roasted or grilled meats.

ingredients

  • finely shredded rommano cheese
  • spaghetti
  • coarsely ground black pepper
  • reserved pasta water

steps

  1. cook spaghetti al dente
  2. add all ingredients to a pan on low heat and stir costantly until combined
  3. that's it
